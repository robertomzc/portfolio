A collection of hand made designs drafted between the ages of 12 and 16 years old. The collection includes all type of vehicles, from Formula 1 cars, to moveable wings fighter jets, passing by small racing cars with batteries and hypercars to compete in LeMans. Some of the were then digitalized into CAD through Solidworks, and some even became part of the decoration of the room after being modeled in 3D using cardboard and Expanded PolyPropilene
